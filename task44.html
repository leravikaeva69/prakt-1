About us

The spacious rooms and suites at the RIVERSIDE Hotel are decorated in a contemporary style using modern and environmentally friendly materials.

They are equipped with orthopedic mattresses and hypoallergenic pillows, a modern ventilation, air conditioning and air purification system, a mini refrigerator, a Nespresso coffee machine and a flat-screen TV.

Free Wi-Fi is available in all rooms. Coffee, tea and water are provided in the rooms free of charge.
